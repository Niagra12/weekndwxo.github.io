About the Albums

Trilogy (2011)

The debut mixtape that introduced the world to The Weeknd’s dark, moody sound—blending R&B with haunting production and raw lyrics.

Beauty Behind the Madness (2015)

This breakout album featured chart-toppers like “Can’t Feel My Face” and showcased his transformation into a mainstream pop icon.

Starboy (2016)

A futuristic, genre-bending album featuring collaborations with Daft Punk. “Starboy” and “I Feel It Coming” cemented his star status.

After Hours (2020)

Filled with retro synths and noir aesthetics, After Hours gave us “Blinding Lights”—a global smash that redefined pop during the pandemic era.

Dawn FM (2022)

This concept album took listeners on a psychedelic radio journey through limbo, blending 80s-inspired sounds with philosophical themes.

Hurry Up Tomorrow (2025)

The highly anticipated follow-up rumored to close his current musical trilogy. Details are under wraps, but the hype is real.
